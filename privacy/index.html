Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 15, 2026

Data collection

We do not collect any personal information from users of our apps or visitors to this site. We do not run analytics, tracking, or behavioral profiling. The apps and this website are designed to work without requiring accounts, sign-ins, or personal data.

If you choose to contact us by email, you may provide your name and email address in that message — we will only use those details to respond to your inquiry and will not retain them longer than necessary to address the request.

Cookies and third parties

We do not use analytics cookies or third-party tracking. The site may load assets from third-party CDNs (e.g., fonts), which may log access for their own operational purposes; we do not control those logs. If we ever integrate third-party services that collect data, we will disclose them here.

Data retention

Because we do not collect personal data, there is nothing we retain for analytics or profiling. Messages you send to us via email are retained only as needed to respond.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, email privacy@lilypad.llc.
